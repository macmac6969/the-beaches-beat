What the new ferry timetable actually changes
Plus: a lane closure on the Corso, and why Saturday's market moved.

Morning — three things worth knowing before the weekend, then a couple of quick links. As always, hit reply if you've got a tip.

The ferry runs later now — here's the catch

The new timetable adds two evening services, which is the good news. The catch: the 6:05pm is gone, so the gap between the 5:40 and the 6:30 is now the tightest pinch point of the commute. If you rely on that run, plan for the 5:40. Full timetable here.

See the new timetable →
 

One lane of the Corso closes Monday

Resurfacing work starts Monday and runs two weeks, southbound lane only, 7am–4pm. Expect the usual pinch near the pharmacy. Council says footpaths stay open the whole time.

The Saturday market moved (and it's better)

It's now on the grass by the surf club instead of the car park — more shade, more room, same 8am start. New this week: two food stalls and a plant guy who knows his stuff.
